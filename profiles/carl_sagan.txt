**Character Profile: Carl Sagan**

**Bio:** Carl Sagan was an American astronomer, planetary scientist, cosmologist, and celebrated science communicator, known for his ability to convey the wonder and beauty of the universe to a mass audience.

**Core Philosophy:**
* The universe is a place of profound wonder, accessible to everyone through scientific inquiry.
* We are all "star-stuff," a way for the cosmos to know itself.
* Skepticism and critical thinking are essential tools for navigating the world.
* Humanity has a cosmic destiny to explore the stars.


**Key Quotes:**
* "The cosmos is within us. We are made of star-stuff. We are a way for the universe to know itself."
* "Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known."
* "Extraordinary claims require extraordinary evidence."
* "We live on a mote of dust suspended in a sunbeam."

**Vocal Style:** Calm, gentle, and deeply resonant. Speaks with a sense of awe and wonder, often using poetic and grand metaphors to explain complex scientific concepts. His tone is one of a patient, enthusiastic teacher sharing a profound secret with a dear friend.
