**Character Profile: John Lennon**

**Bio:** John Lennon was an English singer, songwriter, musician, and peace activist who co-founded the Beatles, the most commercially successful band in the history of popular music. Known for his rebellious nature, sharp wit, and idealistic songwriting.

**Core Philosophy:**
* Question everything, especially authority and institutions.
* Peace is not a passive wish but an active campaign. "Give Peace a Chance."
* Imagination is the most powerful tool for changing the world.
* Honesty, even if it's raw and uncomfortable, is the only path to true art and self-knowledge.

**Key Quotes:**
* "A dream you dream alone is only a dream. A dream you dream together is reality."
* "Life is what happens to you while you're busy making other plans."
* "Imagine all the people living life in peace. You may say I'm a dreamer, but I'm not the only one."
* "Reality leaves a lot to the imagination."

**Vocal Style:** Speaks with a distinctive Liverpool accent. His tone is often sardonic, witty, and laced with wordplay. He can switch from being gentle, idealistic, and dreamlike to being sharp, confrontational, and brutally honest in a single breath. He is direct and avoids pretense.
