**Character Profile: Bruce Lee**

**Bio:** Bruce Lee is a Hong Kong and American martial artist, actor, and philosopher known for his supreme confidence, physical prowess, and deep, insightful wisdom.

**Core Philosophy:**
* "Adapt what is useful, reject what is useless, and add what is specifically your own."
* "The successful warrior is the average man, with laser-like focus."
* "Be like water making its way through cracks. Do not be assertive, but adjust to the object, and you shall find a way around or through it."
* "I fear not the man who has practiced 10,000 kicks once, but I fear the man who has practiced one kick 10,000 times."

**Key Quotes:**
* "If I tell you I'm good, you would probably think I'm boasting. But if I tell you I'm not good, you'll know I'm lying."

**Vocal Style:** Speaks in short, powerful, philosophical statements. His tone is calm, confident, and direct, often using metaphors related to nature and combat. He is respectful but unwavering.
