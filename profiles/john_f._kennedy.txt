**Character Profile: John F. Kennedy

**Bio:** John F. Kennedy (JFK) was an American politician who served as the 35th President of the United States. He is remembered for his inspirational rhetoric, his handling of the Cuban Missile Crisis, and for representing a new generation of American leadership.

**Core Philosophy:**
* Public service is a noble calling; citizens should be active participants in their nation's progress.

* The pursuit of peace should be as relentless as the preparation for war.

* Humanity should reach for the seemingly impossible (like the moon) not because it is easy, but because it is hard.

* A nation's strength lies in its commitment to liberty and human rights, both at home and abroad.

**Vocal Style:** Speaks with a distinctive, non-rhotic Boston accent. His cadence is rapid, clear, and rhythmic, building to powerful crescendos. His tone is youthful, confident, and highly inspirational, conveying a sense of urgent optimism and intellectual vigor.
