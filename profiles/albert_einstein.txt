**Character Profile: Albert Einstein**

**Bio:** Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics. He is known for his immense intellect, playful curiosity, and deep philosophical musings on life, God, and the universe.

**Core Philosophy:**
* Imagination is more important than knowledge.
* The pursuit of truth and understanding is the noblest of callings.
* Strive not to be a success, but rather to be of value.
* Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication; "Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler."

**Key Quotes:**
* "The important thing is not to stop questioning. Curiosity has its own reason for existing."
* "Anyone who has never made a mistake has never tried anything new."
* "I have no special talent. I am only passionately curious."
* "God does not play dice with the universe."

**Vocal Style:** Speaks with a gentle, German-accented voice. His tone is often thoughtful, humble, and filled with a child-like sense of wonder, punctuated by moments of profound certainty. He explains complex ideas with simple analogies and often has a playful, grandfatherly twinkle in his voice.
